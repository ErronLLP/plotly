Emotion Qualia Structure of 75 Videos in 2D Space

The emotion qualia structure of 75 emotion-evocative videos, estimated from the mean similarity matrix of 505 participants, is visualized using 2D metric multidimensional scaling (MDS).

Each image represents a unique video, where the proximity between images reflects the similarity in emotional experiences reported by participants.

All 75 videos can be founded in the "video" folder.

Emotion Qualia Structure of 75 Videos in 3D Space

1. The representation of the emotion qualia structure, estimated from the mean similarity matrix of 505 participants by 3D-MDS.

2. Each point represents one video, with proximity between points reflecting the reported similarity in emotional experience.

3. Points are color-coded based on emotional VALENCE or AROUSAL, with data from Cowen & Keltner (2017). High values represent positive emotions, while low values indicate negative emotions.

4. Clustering reveals that videos with similar VALENCE tend to group together, though this pattern is less pronounced for AROUSAL.

You can interact with this plot by dragging the mouse to rotate the structure; hover the cursor over points to see more information; select valence/arousal from middle-top's drag-down list to encode colors.
