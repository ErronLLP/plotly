Emotion qualia structure of 75 videos in 3D space

This qualia structure estimated from mean similarity matrix of all participants (N = 599). Color is encoded by the values of “valence” and “arousal”, from Cowen & Keltner (2017)

From this figure, we can see video with similar valence and arousal are clustered together. The next step is to compare the qualia structure between alexithymia and typical particpants.
