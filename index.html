Par1: Emotion qualia structure of 75 videos in 3D space

This qualia structure estimated from mean similarity matrix of all participants (N = 599). Color is encoded by the values of “valence” or “arousal”, from Cowen & Keltner (2017)

From this figure, we can see videos with similar valence are clustered together, but in the case of arousal, this pattern is not so salience. The next step is to compare the qualia structure between alexithymia and typical particpants.

Part 3: Correlation between similarity score and score of Bermond-Vorst Alexithymia Questionnaire (BVAQ)

Do Alexithymia Levels Affect Similarity Ratings Between Video Pairs? (N = 599)

# of Entries:

Number of participants tested in a given video pair.

Only the video pairs associated with VIDEO 42 received fewer than 15 participant ratings. This error stems from an error in the Lipeng's honours period.

Correlation:

The correlation between similarity rating and BVAQ score, that is corr(similarity, BVAQ).

Note: The NaN value in the matrix means that all participants gave all similarity ratings for this pair of videos a 7

Significance:

Cells with significant correlation coefficients in the matrix were assigned a value of 1.

Out of a total of 2775 video pairs, 144 pairs showed significant correlation coefficients (5.2%).
